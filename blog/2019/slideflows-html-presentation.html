SlideFlows HTML Presentations

June 1, 2019 — The idea is definitely not a new one and has existed for decades, coming directly from Microsoft, who developed a concept that was not even new for them, which was the ability to display presentations on a computer screen without the need for dozens of different papers, ultimately saving a lot of paper and trees in the process. Going back to this simple idea, we developed SlideFlows, which is an HTML presentation generator, but with emphasis on keeping the presentation as simple and to the point as possible.

SlideFlows is meant for a quick and fast loading presentation. You may share the URL or download the HTML file for distribution among your following. While the techniques of SlideFlows are not as advanced as a Powerpoint presentation, our goal was to keep it unique and focused. No one would complain about having too many features and we do understand that what SlideFlows offers may lack certain features, but our mission was to help accomplish a quick feature presentation available and readable in any browser and on any mobile device where loading was nearly instant and there is no guess work as to whether anyone could see the presentation or not.

We kept it simple and to the point. Simply give your presentation a name to start. Start adding slides that may include text, images, videos, YouTube videos, Vimeo videos, embeds, or audio play. You may also animate how your text looks as well as add custom CSS to your projects. Sometimes a more focused project that gets straight to the point is all you need. Thus, we bring to you a simple, yet powerful tool to generate HTML presentations.

Give SlideFlows a try today!
