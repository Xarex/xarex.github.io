BinCDN WordPress Plugin

April 1, 2020 — After taking inspiration from a previous plugin we built, BunnyCDN+BunnyAPI, we decided to create a new WordPress plugin specifically for our service, BinCDN. If you haven't used BinCDN before, it is a place for you to store your images and most files for a very affordable price. With BinCDN, you can gather analytics about your users with a tracking code, allow for comments, or even use your own CNAME doamin so that the files look as if they are being served right from your own domain. BinCDN also encourages sharing with your social media networks.

This plugin is very simple to use and can be used immediately once activate with the BinCDN API key provided within your account. You can store as many files as your plan will allow. Last month, we had an ongoing deal for a 1 TB plan priced at $49 per year or just around $4 per month. This is a deal that you could not find anywhere else. We may offer it again in the future. Fortunately, it is just the beginning of April, and we still have the deal going on until the end of the week. While this was published on April 1, 2020, we are not joking. We really are offering 1 TB plans for $49 per year, but the catch is, of course, that you have to commit to the plan for 1 year. Even if you don't use it all, you can still use it for storage or sharing purposes.

You can search the WordPress repository for BinCDN or download it from this link: . There are not too many configuration features other than to save bandwidth, which is unlimited on BinCDN anyway, but the feature is still there to save your website from having to download files. Other than that, you can force push your WordPress Media library to BinCDN if you wish or upload a single file or multiple files at a time. We hope BinCDN and the BinCDN plugin make your life as easy as it has made ours.
